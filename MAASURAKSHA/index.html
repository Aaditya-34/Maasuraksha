Core Technology

Three Pillars of Continuous Care

Every component is designed for the realities of rural India — low connectivity, diverse literacy levels, and limited specialist access.

📡

IoT Wearable Band

Continuously measures Heart Rate, SpO₂, Blood Pressure, Temperature, and activity. Low-power BLE with 7-day battery life. Water-resistant for all-day wear.

HR + HRV SpO₂ BP Temp Activity
📱

Low-Literacy Mobile App

Icon-first UI with voice prompts in Hindi and regional languages. Works on 2G. Designed with village women and ASHA workers through co-design sessions.

Voice Alerts Offline-first Multilingual
🧠

GenAI Clinical Intelligence

Goes beyond threshold alerts — analyzes physiological trends over time to predict high-risk pregnancies early. Generates clear explanations for patients and doctors alike.

Trend Analysis Risk Prediction Explainable AI

Conditions Detected Early

💉

Preeclampsia

Rising systolic/diastolic BP and HRV changes flag risk weeks before clinical presentation.

🩸

Anemia

Persistent low SpO₂ and reduced activity patterns indicate hemoglobin deficiency.

🍬

Gestational Diabetes

HR variability and activity patterns combined with vitals suggest GDM risk for targeted screening.

System Flow

How MaaSuraksha Works

A seamless three-stage loop from continuous sensing to actionable insight.

1
📡

Sense & Stream

The IoT band continuously captures vital signs and sends encrypted data to the cloud via the patient's phone — even with intermittent 2G connectivity through local caching.

2
🧠

AI Risk Analysis

Cloud AI analyzes physiological trends over time — not just instant thresholds. GenAI generates plain-language health summaries and clinical reports personalized for each stakeholder.

3
🔔

Targeted Action

Patients receive voice alerts in their language. ASHA workers get field checklists. Doctors receive prioritized clinical summaries — enabling timely, hierarchical intervention.

The Problem

  • •Physiological warning signs appear early but rarely monitored continuously
  • •Doctors face data overload from episodic manual readings
  • •Patients struggle to understand medical alerts
🎯

Our Novelty

Unlike typical systems that trigger simple threshold alerts, MaaSuraksha analyzes physiological trends over time to predict high-risk pregnancies early, then uses GenAI to transform complex data into clear, personalized explanations.

🌱

Our Promise

Shift maternal care from reactive emergency response to proactive, preventive support — bringing safer pregnancies and healthier babies to rural communities across India.
