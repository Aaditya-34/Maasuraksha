Integrations

Works with your existing devices

No new hardware needed. MaaSuraksha connects to the wearable you already own — or works perfectly with manual entry.

⌚
Apple Watch

Sync heart rate, blood oxygen, and activity data via Apple HealthKit. Works with Series 4 and above.

● Coming Soon
🏃
Fitbit

Connect via Fitbit Web API to pull heart rate, SpO₂, sleep quality, and daily activity data automatically.

● In Development
📱
Google Fit

Works with any Android wearable or phone sensor data via Google Fit API — Garmin, Samsung, and more.

● Planned
✍️
Manual Entry

No wearable? No problem. Enter your BP, weight, and vitals manually after each check-up. Works for everyone.

● Always Available
The Process

From data to peace of mind

Four simple steps from syncing your wearable to getting actionable health guidance.

1
Connect & Sync

Link your Apple Watch, Fitbit, or enter vitals manually. Your data syncs securely to your health record.

2
AI Analysis

Gemini AI analyzes your vitals, symptoms, and pregnancy stage to compute your real-time risk score.

3
Smart Alerts

If something needs attention, you and your doctor are notified instantly with a clear explanation.

4
Care Coordination

Your doctor sees a risk-prioritized dashboard and can act fast — from anywhere, at any time.
